Our Journey Starts in Los Angeles

When most people think of LA the last thing they think of is good hiking, but LA has some very scenic and remote hikes

Not to mention LA has wildlife!

Our first stop is Cheseburo Canyon as we explore rolling hills and caves!

Our entrance starts out with an easy trek then onward to slow decents and short climbs

This hike has the potential to be very long, or it can be short. The choice is yours

Keep an eye out for caves, they are scattered throughout the LA area

Journey north as we explore Rocky Peak Park

This hike can be more intense with steep climbs but the views in all directions are well worth it

Most entrances are right off the 118 freeway

Our last stop in LA takes us to Aliso Canyon Trail Park

This hike is easy with gradual climbs and smooth trails. It is also surrounded by suburbs, but still offers all the glory of nature

Don’t be threatened, they are just rabbits


Next we journey north to the Sierra Nevada Mountains

Mt. Whitney greets you as head north on highway 395
