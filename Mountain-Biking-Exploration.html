Lets start by exploring Harmon Canyon in Ventura, California

This canyon is full of fun, flow trails. Some would consider this a short ride of around 10 miles

You can either make your way to the top for a great view or stay low and still ride some great trails

On weekdays Harmon Canyon is relatively uncrowded. However, in the afternoons watch out for hikers and other people on the trial

This canyon is has few emanates like bathrooms but it does have a bike service station at the entrance
